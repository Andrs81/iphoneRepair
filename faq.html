iFixOrlando FAQ

Check out our FAQ. Still don't find what you need?

Contact us with your question and we'll answer you shortly!

Who are the iFixOrlando techs?

Our technicians are smart phone guru's who are enthusiastic about saving customers in need. All of our technicians have been through an extensive interview process, industry level quiz and full background check.

How do I set up a repair?

Please use this link to start the repair process. Simply choose your device type, device issue and enter your contact information. We will then set up a repair meeting for you with our tech at a convenient time and location of your choice.

I work long hours and do not have time to wait, can you come to my office?

Yes! We can gladly meet you at your office, home, gym or even a nearby coffee place! We will fix the device in front of you and most repairs are completed within 30 - 40 minutes.

What is the benefit of using iFixOrlando?

Imagine traveling to a repair store, finding parking, and being separated from your device for hours. Our service alleviates all the pain points of the conventional device repair process, while ensuring your data is kept private and secure. Our online process is easy to use and our service is affordable on any budget. We guarantee all repairs for life!

How long until I can get my device fixed?

While we aim to provide a same-day repair meeting, scheduling ultimately depends on both our schedule and yours. We schedule based on your preferred location and timing as well as our technicians' availability

After I set up my appointment, how can I contact iFixOrlando?

You can call us at 321-355-4648 or email us at support@iFixOrlando.com

Will the iFixOrlando technician contact me with an ETA?

Yes! They will text or call you when they are on the way to save the day!

Do you provide a warranty on repairs?

Yes! All of our parts and services come with a Lifetime Warranty that covers failure of workmanship and materials for the life of its products. Normal wear and tear and damages as of a result of the user is not covered by the warranty.

How do I pay for this service?

Simply pay our technician AFTER the repair is complete using the technician's mobile phone. We accept all major credit and debit cards.

How do I become a iFixOrlando tech?

We are always looking to add qualified techinicians to our network. To apply, simply click here to fill out an application.
